KIOWAGRAINS

FILM • LIGHT • MEMORY

KiowaGrains is my own visual journal shaped through grief of the woman I love and a losing battle with cancer, other forms of loss, and the quiet process of remembering. Using mainly analog and some digital photography, my art explores nostalgia, solitude, fleeting light, and the emotional textures carried within seemingly ordinary moments. The resulting work is what learning to see the world again looks like — through my eyes. -Dylan G. Saile
