KIOWAGRAINS

FILM • LIGHT • MEMORY

KiowaGrains is a visual journal shaped through grief, loss, and the quiet process of remembering. Using analog and digital photography, the work explores nostalgia, solitude, fleeting light, and the emotional texture carried within ordinary moments. The resulting work is what learning to see the world again looks like — through my eyes.
